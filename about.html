About Us

Focused expertise in British curriculum

An education advisory firm helping schools deliver internationally recognised British education.

What We Do

British Education Consultants is an education advisory and school transformation firm. We specialise in the British National Curriculum and help schools transition from local systems to internationally benchmarked British education.

Our work covers three main areas: training teachers to deliver British curriculum effectively, developing school leaders through UK-recognised qualifications, and providing advisory services for schools looking to gain international accreditation.

We work primarily with schools in Africa, the Middle East, and Asia who want to offer internationally recognised education to their students.

Our Approach

Practical, context-appropriate solutions

We believe every school is different. We work to understand your specific situation before recommending a path forward.

Our programmes are designed to build internal capacity. We're not here to create dependency—we want your teachers and leaders to be confident and capable on their own.

We measure success by the lasting improvements we help create in teaching and learning, not just the programmes we deliver.

Our Purpose

Guided by clear mission and values

Mission

To equip educators and institutions with the skills, knowledge, and support needed to deliver the British National Curriculum with confidence and competence.

Vision

To be a trusted partner for schools seeking to deliver British education excellence, helping them build sustainable capacity for quality teaching and learning.

Values

Excellence in standards. Integrity in practice. Commitment to continuous improvement. Respect for local context.
