About BEC

Focused expertise in British curriculum implementation

British Education Consultants is an education advisory firm specialising in the British National Curriculum. We work with schools looking to offer internationally benchmarked education.

Our team has hands-on experience in curriculum development, teacher training, and school leadership across UK institutions. We bring this expertise to schools in Africa, the Middle East, and Asia.
