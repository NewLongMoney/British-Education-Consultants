Our Services

Practical support for schools

Training, advisory, and development services for British curriculum implementation.

01

Teacher Training Programmes

We deliver structured training programmes for teachers transitioning to British National Curriculum. Our programmes cover:

  • Understanding the BNC framework and expectations
  • Subject-specific pedagogy and best practices
  • Assessment methods and moderation
  • Differentiation and inclusive teaching
  • Practical classroom strategies

Training can be delivered on-site at your school or through blended programmes combining in-person and online components.

02

Leadership Development

Building strong school leadership is essential for sustainable improvement. We offer:

  • NPQ (National Professional Qualification) programmes
  • Headteacher and senior leader development
  • Middle leader training
  • Governance training for school boards
  • Strategic planning support

Our leadership programmes are aligned with UK standards and help leaders build the skills needed to drive school improvement.

03

School Advisory Services

For schools looking to transition to or improve their delivery of British curriculum, we provide:

  • Feasibility assessment and planning
  • Curriculum mapping and implementation
  • Accreditation support (Cambridge, etc.)
  • Quality assurance and academic audits
  • Policy development

We work with schools at all stages—from initial exploration through to full accreditation and ongoing quality assurance.

04

Continuous Professional Development

For schools already delivering international curriculum, we offer ongoing CPD to keep staff current:

  • Subject-specific workshops
  • Curriculum updates and changes
  • Assessment and moderation training
  • Teaching and learning reviews
  • Cambridge PDQ qualification

CPD can be delivered as one-off sessions or as part of an annual programme tailored to your school's needs.

05

Teacher Recruitment Support

Finding qualified teachers for international schools can be challenging. We help with:

  • Candidate sourcing and screening
  • Qualification verification
  • Interview support
  • Induction programme design

We connect schools with qualified educators who have experience in British curriculum delivery.
